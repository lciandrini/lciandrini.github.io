Teaching

In September 2021 we launched a new Master cursus, named quantitative biology (qbio). This interdisciplinary curriculum is mainly based on practical curses and student projects. Here you can find the website of the curriculum. Visit the website and contact us if you need further information.

I am co-coordinator of the iGEM Montpellier team. We started an iGEM team in 2018 with the project Vagineering, which aim was to develop a non-hormonal contraceptive. In 2019 the team developed a a new molecular tool for specific protein degradation, KARMA. In 2020 the team worked on PHAGENT, a project on phages hijacking a particular cancer microbiome. In 2022, with our project SHELL’LOCK, we were 1st runner up in the overgraduate category (2nd team among all the overgrad teams).
