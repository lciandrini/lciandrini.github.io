Luca Ciandrini’s group

We are part of the Biomolecular Modelling group at the Centre de Biologie Structurale (CBS), University of Montpellier.



Our work combines theoretical, computational, and experimental approaches to explore the molecular mechanisms underlying gene expression and cell growth.

By integrating methods from statistical mechanics and biophysics, we aim to uncover the fundamental principles driving cellular resource allocation and how these shape growth and physiology.
