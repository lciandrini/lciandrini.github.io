Group Organisation

Here a few guidelines for our group organisation.

GitHub repositories

We have a private GitHub repository for sharing image files, presentations, etc etc. Ask Luca to be invited.

In addition, projects may have their own repository.

Communication: Slack and email

We use slack for quick communication and chatting. Ask Luca to be invited to the channel.

I’d suggest to use your @etu.umontpellier.fr or @umontpellier.fr account, and please check it regularly.

Be careful: your working ours may not coincide with the ones from others. If you send or receive a message at 2am or during the weekend you shouldn’t expect an immediate answer (or answer immediately if you cannot).
