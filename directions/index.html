How to Reach Us

We are located at the Centre de Biologie Structurale (CBS) in Montpellier, France. Below, you’ll find detailed directions to help you reach us.

Address

Centre de Biologie Structurale (CBS)
29 rue de Navacelles
34090 Montpellier
France

  • Google Maps link
  • Phone: +33 (0)4 67 41 77 00
  • Website: CBS Website

Access by Public Transport

Montpellier’s public transport system is managed by TaM.

  1. From Montpellier Saint-Roch Train Station:
    • Take Tram Line 1 (direction “Mosson”).
    • Stop at Occitanie.
    • Walk approximately 2 minutes to the CBS building.
  2. From Montpellier Méditerranée Airport:
    • Take the airport shuttle (Bus Line 120) to Place de l’Europe tram stop.
    • From Place de l’Europe, take Tram Line 1 (direction “Mosson”).
    • Stop at Occitanie.
    • Walk approximately 2 minutes to the CBS building.

Access by Car

  • Parking:
    • Visitor parking spaces are available at the CBS building (2nd gate).

Feel free to contact us if you need further assistance in planning your visit.
